Frequently Asked Questions

Institute of Chartered Secretaries of Bangladesh. It offers professional degree of Chartered Secretary for development of the nation. It is a statutory body under Ministry of Commerce, Government of the People's Republic of Bangladesh.

CS is a professional degree in the field of secretarial and governance practice. You will understand the difference between academic and professional degrees while you are in ICSB. Those who complete CS, receive ACS membership initially and with time and following requirements, they become FCS. In Bangladesh, CS qualification is offered by Institute of Chartered Secretaries of Bangladesh (ICSB).

Not exactly same. ICAB and ICMAB are the accounting professional bodies, whereas ICSB is the only recognized professional body to develop, promote and regulate the profession of Chartered/Company Secretaries in Bangladesh. In broader aspect to enhance Corporate Governance, ICSB provides degree in Chartered Secretaryship.

ICSB regulates and develop the profession of Chartered Secretaries in order to promote and establish statutory disciplines and conduct the Company matter and management effectively in line with Corporate Governance and Code of Conduct. It regularly arranges Continuing Development Program (CPD) to enhance the efficiency of the secretarial profession.

Chartered Secretarial Course is of international standard based on Chartered Secretaries Institute worldwide specially UK, Canada, Australia, Hong Kong and Indian Syllabus. There are two parts of CS study i.e. Certificate Level and Professional Level. Most of the courses are related to secretariatship, laws, regulations, accounting, taxation, auditing and governance studies etc.

To pass certificate level, students need to complete all those subjects. Students can take and pass subject wise according to their capacity.
